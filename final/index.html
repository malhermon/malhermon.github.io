Our Purpose

Our non-profit organization has over 1,100 voluntary members and it was founded in 1907. Our members are locals of the Temple area. We appreciate them because it is through their donations and contributions that we are able to keep growing. In exchange, we assist and motivate our members by offering them programs and services that help their businesses grow.
