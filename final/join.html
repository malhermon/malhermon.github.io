Membership

With over 1,100 voluntary members who offer their expertise and invest their time and money into improving our community, economy, and cultural well-being; the Temple Chamber of Commerce is excited to receive more volunteers.

Any business that is interesting in developing in our area is eligible because we know they bring more jobs, more money, and a better quality of life for the Temple residents. The dues structure allows for owners of the smallest businesses to become members.

Benefits

Network

  • Meet new people and businesses.
  • Opportunity to market your own products.
  • Get new reviews and improve visibility.
  • Increase exposure.

Invest in your region

  • Improve the quality of life of the community.
  • Address the opportunities and challenges of the city.
  • Help creating new jobs for the people.
  • Safeguard our municipal resources.

Grow your business

  • Learn from other experts.
  • Opportunities for mentorship programs.
  • Learn strategies to resolve common issues.
  • Strengthen your business and employees.
